Featured Project

Great Smoky Mountains / Breath of the Wild Map

Great Smoky Mountains National Park

Styled after The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

This QGIS project reimagines the topography of Great Smoky Mountains National Park through the visual language of Nintendo's Breath of the Wild — a game known for its iconic hand-drawn map aesthetic. Custom contour intervals, a Hyrule-inspired color palette, and styled points of interest translate real elevation data into something that feels simultaneously faithful to the landscape and evocative of the game world.

The project demonstrates proficiency in custom QGIS symbology, contour styling, layer management, and cartographic design thinking. It also reflects a core conviction: that map aesthetics are never superficial — they shape how readers emotionally and cognitively engage with geographic space.
